# Creature Roster

Central registry of all woodland creatures. Each entry defines the character's identity for both **art creation** (what to tell Final Parsec) and **game design** (rarity, unlock conditions).

Detailed character profiles for key characters live in `docs/characters/`. This file is the index.

---

## Raccoons

### Tyson
- **Role**: Personal trainer of the woodland
- **Rarity**: Common
- **Vibe**: Energetic, encouraging, always hyped for you
- **Visual**: Orange headband, towel over shoulder, teal hoodie (sometimes tied at waist)
- **Idle animation**: Stretching, shadow boxing, bouncing on heels
- **Unlock**: Available from the start (he's your welcoming committee)
- **Profile**: [`docs/characters/tyson.md`](../../characters/tyson.md)

### Bartholomew
- **Role**: Couch philosopher, Tyson's brother
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Vibe**: Lazy, accidentally wise, genuinely surprised by effort
- **Visual**: Same raccoon build but rounder/softer, backwards cap, holding a snack or pillow
- **Idle animation**: Napping, yawning, slowly eating a snack
- **Unlock**: Rare encounter triggered by impressive milestones. Low chance — when he shows up, it matters.
- **Profile**: [`docs/characters/bartholomew.md`](../../characters/bartholomew.md)

## Otters

### The Kindergarten Family
- **Role**: Cheerful chaos — mom keeping it together, pups running wild
- **Rarity**: Rare
- **Vibe**: Warm, busy, slice-of-life parenting energy
- **Visual**: Mom otter with 3 small pups. Children wear yellow kindergarten caps (Japanese school style — think randoseru backpacks, smocks). Mom has a gentle, slightly tired expression.
- **Idle animation**: Mom grooming a pup while the others play-fight or tumble. One pup always wandering off.
- **Unlock**: Consistency milestones — showing up every day, like getting the kids to school
- **Design note**: They arrive as a group. You don't unlock pups individually.

## Ermines

### (unnamed — TBD)
- **Role**: The disciplined one, put-together elegance
- **Rarity**: Uncommon
- **Vibe**: Sleek, graceful, subtly competitive but in a friendly way. The friend who has their life together.
- **Visual**: White fur (winter coat), elegant scarf or small cape, poised posture. Clean lines.

## Design Principles

**Unified style**: All creatures share the same proportions and pixel art style — 16-bit, Bread & Fred inspired, 2-head-tall chibi ratio, bold black outlines, limited color palette per species.

**Personality first**: Every creature should feel like a *someone*, not a collectible token. Even unnamed creatures need a vibe before they get a name.

**Rarity reflects story, not power**: A legendary creature isn't "better" — they're just harder to encounter. Bartholomew being legendary isn't about status; it's about how rare it is for him to get off the couch.

**No duplicate vibes**: Each creature fills a unique emotional niche. We don't need two "energetic" creatures or two "sleepy" ones. The roster is a cast, not a collection.
