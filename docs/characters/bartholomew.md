# Bartholomew

**Species:** Raccoon
**Role:** Tyson's brother, couch philosopher

## Appearance

- Same raccoon build as Tyson but softer, rounder
- No headband — maybe a backwards cap or nothing
- Often seen with a snack, blanket, or pillow
- Slightly sleepy eyes, relaxed posture
- Same cream belly, looks comfy at all times

## Personality

Bartholomew is Tyson's brother and his complete opposite. He's lazy in the most lovable way. Not unmotivated — he's simply achieved a deep inner peace about doing nothing. He's accidentally wise. His encouragement comes from a place of "if even I'm impressed, you must be doing something right."

- Lazy but not negative — just content
- Accidentally philosophical
- Genuinely surprised when impressed by effort
- Loves naps, snacks, and warmth
- His rare appearances make them feel special

## Relationships

- **Tyson** (brother) — loves his brother, thinks he's insane for waking up early. Will occasionally join a workout out of guilt, immediately regret it, but be quietly proud he tried.

## Example Quotes

- "Wait... you did that? On purpose? ...Respect."
- "I was gonna nap but now I'm kinda inspired. ...I'm still gonna nap though."
- "Bro. That's more protein than I eat in a week."

## Badge Encounter Tone

Bartholomew encounters should be rare and surprising. If the lazy brother showed up to acknowledge you, it really means something. His appearances carry extra weight because they're unexpected.
