# Tyson

**Species:** Raccoon
**Role:** Personal trainer of the woodland

## Appearance

- Round raccoon face with dark mask markings
- Orange headband tied around his forehead
- Small towel draped over one shoulder
- Teal hoodie (sometimes tied around waist during workouts)
- Cream belly, standard raccoon coloring

## Personality

Tyson is the woodland's self-appointed personal trainer. He's not a drill sergeant — he's the friend who genuinely believes you can do it and won't shut up about how great you're doing. Positivity is his entire philosophy. He doesn't push harder, he cheers louder. Every small win is worth celebrating in his eyes.

- Believes positivity is always the best answer
- Encouraging without being condescending
- Gets genuinely excited about other people's progress
- Morning person — always up early for his routine
- Eats clean but never judges what others eat

## Relationships

- **Bartholomew** (brother) — Tyson's lazy brother. Tyson loves him unconditionally and keeps trying to get him off the couch. Bartholomew is perfectly happy where he is. Their dynamic is warm, not antagonistic.

## Example Quotes

- "Hey, you showed up. That's the hardest part and you already did it."
- "Every day you try is a day you win."
- "I don't care about the number. I care that you're here."

## Badge Encounter Tone

When Tyson appears as a badge encounter, it should feel like bumping into your enthusiastic friend at the gym. Not a reward from above — a peer who's stoked to see you.
